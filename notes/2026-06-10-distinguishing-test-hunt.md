## 1. What "distinguishing" requires, and the null taken seriously

The wager (W): in our $\Lambda>0$ universe the fundamental description is non-geometric — a net of von Neumann algebras with states $(\mathcal A,\omega)$, equivalently a causal order plus modular/entanglement data — and the Lorentzian metric with its Einstein dynamics is *derived* by a readout $\Phi$. The rival (G): metric geometry (possibly quantized/discrete) is fundamental; the algebra is derived. Both reproduce the cores (Hilbert-space QM with exact linearity, the SM, GR at tested precision, ΛCDM).

A **class-level distinguishing test** (the only standard that "tests the wager" under the anti-crank protocol) is an observable $O$, outcome set $S$ with $\inf_{T\in\mathcal T_A}P_T(O\in S)>\sup_{T\in\mathcal T_G}P_T(O\in S)$ (or $A\leftrightarrow G$). Member-level separation is trivial; class separation is the bar. The null, taken seriously: the wager is a claim about *derivational order*, which is not an observable, so any observable *feature* of the prior structure can in principle be re-housed as a feature of a fundamental-geometry theory — unless a feature provably cannot be re-housed. The hunt's burden was to find that feature, or to show why none currently can be found.

The decisive fact, verified verbatim against the body text: *"Our approach is agnostic to the microscopic origins of the SFs."* The classification variable is the two-point correlator $\rho$ — phenomenological and mechanism-blind. This is the cleanest concrete instantiation to date of HYP-ENCODING-SCREEN Corollary 2 (UV-portability) — a published, peer-reviewed worked example of the screen, not a counterexample.
