# Convergence / completeness audit — how far from a conclusion?

Status: Active audit — headline verdict UNCHANGED (PARTIAL coherence; wager not-yet-physics); new-insight trend DIMINISHING; recommendation HYBRID.
Last updated: 2026-06-08
Iteration: 4

This note steps back from the object-level physics and asks a meta-question: **how much more analytical work is left before the "state of the question" is stable, and how far is the actual object-level verdict (a confirmed universal theory)?** It audits the iteration-1 → 4 trajectory for saturation, estimates two distinct distances-to-conclusion, lists the finite set of moves that could still move the standing verdict, logs the internal-consistency residue, and gives a resourcing recommendation. It does **not** re-derive the iteration-4 result; that lives in [the SO(d+1,1)-family attempt note](2026-06-08-iter4-dS-firstlaw-SO-family-attempt.md). The one-line headline: **the program is converging, not advancing — each iteration's marginal yield is increasingly a sharper obstruction or a self-correction rather than a new positive structure.** [INFERENCE, high]

## Distance to an ANALYTICAL conclusion

**CLOSE — roughly 1–3 more focused iterations to analytical saturation** (a stable, defensible "state of the question"). [INFERENCE, high] The analytical conclusion is already largely in hand and stable across iterations 2 → 3 → 4:

1. **"Is a universal theory possible?"** is answered in *disambiguated* form:
   - **(A) a consistent unified GR+QFT framework: POSSIBLE, high confidence** — AdS/CFT is an existence proof; the catalogued clashes are domain-mismatches, not contradictions (see [../GAPS_AND_CONTRADICTIONS.md](../GAPS_AND_CONTRADICTIONS.md)).
   - **(B) a final substrate: possible but not demonstrably so** — a Wilsonian tower-with-no-bottom is a live alternative.
   - **(C) a parameter-fixing complete theory: least likely.**
2. **The one central bet is named precisely:** *causal/algebraic/entanglement structure precedes metric geometry, true of our $\Lambda>0$ universe.*
3. **WHY it is not-yet-physics is established and survived every attack:** it **ENCODES** geometry, it does not **GENERATE** it (every rigorous instance presupposes a fixed background with Killing/conformal symmetry — Bisognano–Wichmann/CHM circularity), and it has **no distinguishing near-term test** (every live channel tests the gate, the target, or the cores — see [next section](#distance-to-an-object-level-confirmed-theory)).
4. **What experiment must deliver is catalogued:** BMV $m^2$-vs-$m^3$; objective-collapse bounds; $w(z)$.

What **remains analytically** is a short, finite, *named* list (see [What would change the standing verdict](#what-would-change-the-standing-verdict)). Iteration 4 just shrank it by (provisionally) closing the single highest-leverage theory target — OP-41 via its only un-foreclosed route — which is exactly the kind of move that drives saturation. The remaining named problems — **OP-48 / OP-GPT-TYPE3**, **OP-44 / OP-CP1**, and the **OP-46 Bisognano–Wichmann-breaking (clause-(iii)) construction** — are the ones whose resolution would saturate the analytical question. After they are settled, marginal STRUCTURAL insight goes to near-zero and the program becomes a watch-list. [INFERENCE, high]

## Distance to an OBJECT-LEVEL (confirmed) theory

**NOT FORECASTABLE — and this is a stronger obstruction than mere distance.** [INFERENCE, high] These two distances must not be conflated: the analytical question is nearly settled; the object-level question is not even *schedulable*.

An actual confirmed universal theory is gated by two things the program cannot schedule:

1. **The ~15-order-of-magnitude empirical desert** between accessible energies ($\sim\mathrm{TeV}$) and the Planck scale ($M_{\rm Pl}\approx 1.22\times 10^{19}\,\mathrm{GeV}$) — no accessible experiment probes the regime where GR and QFT must be reconciled.
2. **More fundamentally, the central wager currently has NO distinguishing experimental test even in principle.** The falsifiability portfolio (in [../FINDINGS.md](../FINDINGS.md) and [the iter-3 synthesis](2026-06-08-iter3-synthesis.md)) shows that every live channel tests something *adjacent* to the wager, never the wager itself:

| BMV / QGEM ($m^2$ vs $m^3$ witness) | Program A's logical **GATE** (is gravity non-classical?) | 2030s–2040s | matter-wave / optomechanics mass scale |
| Objective-collapse (CSL / Diósi–Penrose) | status of a fixed **CORE** (is exact linearity / unitarity false?) | ~2035 | same mass-scale technology |
| $w(z)$ (DESI DR3+/Euclid/Roman) | the program's **TARGET** (is the universe eternal dS?) | 2–7 yr | survey cadence |
| LIV / EP nulls | status of a fixed **CORE** (is local Lorentz exact?) | ~2035 | next-gen detectors |

**None tests geometry-from-algebra itself.** So even optimistic timelines on the *enabling* technology — matter-wave / levitated-optomechanics mass scale, the shared bottleneck, ~5–15 yr — would only open the **GATE**, not confirm the theory. The object-level conclusion is therefore not merely far but **non-forecastable**: it lacks a *defined experimental decision procedure*, which is a stronger obstruction than distance. ## Recommendation (hybrid) + distance estimate

**Recommendation: HYBRID.** [confidence: high]

Iteration 4 mostly sharpened obstructions and confirmed convergence — and the audit should say so plainly. It produced **one genuinely new high-value NEGATIVE result** (the dS cardinality no-go), settled one open problem as a sharp disjunction (OP-48), promoted one open obstruction toward a near-no-go (the clause-(iii) / CKV-vacuity issue), found **zero new positive structure**, and stabilized the PARTIAL / not-yet-physics verdict for the *third consecutive iteration*. New-insight trend is **DIMINISHING**.

Recommended allocation for the remaining ~1–3 iterations:

1. **ATTACK the three named, genuinely-decidable analytical problems.** OP-48 point (c) (a state-/collar-free composition surrogate on a Type III$_1$ factor) and OP-44 / OP-CP1 (nonperturbative fate of the crossed product) each saturate a gap *either way*; the OP-46 non-symmetric-modular-flow construction is the **only** move that would change the headline verdict (turn "encode" into "generate"). Also attempt the two un-foreclosed dS routes (non-unitary dS/CFT pseudo-entropy; Jacobson small-balls inside a single Type II$_1$ algebra).
2. **DO the two cheap documentation passes:** propagate the unfinished red-team fixes (the "heuristic-not-program" relabel and the Caves-Fuchs-Manne-Renes spelling) into the iter-2 synthesis note; reconcile/flag the remaining date drift.
3. **SIMULTANEOUSLY stand up lightweight experiment-tracking** for the four empirical channels (BMV $m^2$/$m^3$; collapse bounds ~2035; $w(z)$ DESI DR3+/Euclid 2–7 yr; LIV/EP), since those — not further analysis — gate the object-level verdict, and none currently tests the geometry-from-algebra wager directly.

**Rationale:** pure continue-attacking risks diminishing returns; pure pivot-to-tracking prematurely abandons 2–3 genuinely decidable named problems. Hence hybrid. [confidence: high]
