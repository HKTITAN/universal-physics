### The fatal misidentification (the gap object)

The iteration-9 referee correction placed the natural Gram operator $G$ in **the commutant of the modular flow on the representation Hilbert space**, $\{\Delta^{it}\}'$, and located the gap as $\{\Delta\}''\subsetneq\{\Delta^{it}\}'$ — strictly larger **because the spectrum of $\Delta$ on $H$ is degenerate** (the referee's own words: "the abelian algebra generated by Delta … differ because the modular spectrum is degenerate (numerics: 12 spectral degeneracies in a 4×4 toy)"). That gap is between two operator algebras **on $H$**, driven by *eigenvalue multiplicity of $\Delta$*.

The submission silently re-reads this gap as a gap involving the **modular centralizer** $\mathcal M_\omega=\mathcal M\cap\{\Delta^{it}\}'$ — an algebra **inside $\mathcal M$** — and then "closes" it by invoking ergodicity of the Bisognano–Wichmann vacuum ($\mathcal M_\omega=\mathbb C$). $$\{\Delta\}''\ \subsetneq\ \{\Delta^{it}\}'\quad\text{(on }H\text{, the actual iter-9 gap)};\qquad \mathcal M_\omega=\mathcal M\cap\{\Delta^{it}\}'\quad\text{(inside }\mathcal M\text{)}.$$
