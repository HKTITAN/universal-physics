## 5. Neutrino-mass cosmology — proposed NEW watchlist row

[ESTABLISHED] DESI DR2 BAO + CMB (arXiv:2503.14744, verified): $\sum m_\nu < 0.0642$ eV (95%, ΛCDM) — below the inverted-ordering minimum ($\approx 0.10$ eV) and pressing on the normal-ordering oscillation floor ($\sum m_\nu \gtrsim 0.059$ eV).
